The Start

Back in 2013, I made a decision that changed everything—I chose tech. It wasn’t trendy, and I wasn’t even sure it was right for me. But something clicked.

Education

I studied Computer Science at JUST University in Jordan (class of 2020), and after a deep dive into NodeJS, I landed my first job as a backend developer.

Ambition

The corporate world treated me well, but I’ve always wanted more than a stable job. I wanted to grow, create, and redefine what “developer” means to me.

Reality Check

Like many, I’ve dealt with imposter syndrome. I used to shrink my identity to just "backend"—but I’ve learned that what we build and how we think goes far beyond a label.

Purpose

This space is my reset button. A place to document, experiment, and share the journey of building, learning, and evolving in tech—and maybe beyond it.

Join me in the journey.
